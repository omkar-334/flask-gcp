About Us

Loyola High School is a renowned educational institution located in Bodhan, District Nizamabad, Telangana, India. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering the holistic development of our students.

Our Branches

  • Mahalaxmi Mandir Campus
  • Saraswathi Nagar Campus (with Hostel Facility)

Academics

We offer classes from Nursery to 10th Class in English Medium, providing a strong foundation for academic excellence.

Facilities

  • Spacious and well-equipped classrooms
  • Modern laboratories for practical experiments
  • Computer labs for digital literacy
  • Sports facilities for physical fitness and teamwork
  • Library with a vast collection of books
  • Bus facility for students in both branches
  • Hostel facility at Saraswathi Nagar Campus
